BIOGRAPHY

The Artist Behind The Canvas

"Art is not what I see, but what I make others see. I blend classical mediums with state-of-the-art technological canvases to design absolute experiences."

Sayantani is a pioneering visual storyteller whose practice thrives in the captivating grey area between fine physical art and high-definition graphic design. With years spent mastering fine paint handling, charcoal anatomy, and gold-leaf guilding techniques, she bridges traditional aesthetic depth with a futuristic digital design language.

Each piece is conceptualized as an emotional sanctuary, where layers of acrylic flow or geometric layouts offer viewers a momentary escape from the chaotic modern digital cycle into an elevated, luxury aesthetic world.

2022
